About Victor "Bicho" Carrera

Victor “Bicho” Carrera is a world-renowned Chilean paragliding acro pilot and elite Red Bull athlete, celebrated for his exceptional precision, fearless creativity, and boundary-pushing aerial maneuvers. Born in 1996 in Chile, Bicho developed a passion for flight at an early age, taking to the skies for the first time at just 11 years old. His natural talent and dedication quickly became apparent, and by the age of 15, he was already competing in international acrobatic paragliding events. Over the years, Bicho has carved out a name for himself in the global acro paragliding scene through his bold, artistic flying style and innovative trick combinations that continually raise the bar for the sport. His performances are characterized not only by technical mastery but also by a distinctive flair that captures the imagination of audiences and judges alike. As a member of the prestigious Red Bull athlete team, Bicho travels the world performing in competitions, shows, and air displays, inspiring a new generation of pilots and thrill-seekers. With a career built on passion, discipline, and a relentless pursuit of perfection, Victor "Bicho" Carrera continues to push the limits of what's possible in the skies.

Airshows by Victor "Bicho" Carrera

Bicho delivers breathtaking aerial performances with paramotor, acro, and nightshows. His unique combination of precision, creativity, and style has wowed audiences across the globe. From dazzling LED-lit night displays to jaw-dropping stunts over city skylines, Bicho's shows bring sky-high energy to every event — whether it's a concert, festival, or sports venue.
